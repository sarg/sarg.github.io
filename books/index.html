Digital Minimalism

It was a little bit awkward at first that the author itself doesn’t struggle with internet addiction. He doesn’t use facebook for example. But it is completely balanced with a thoughtful approach and sound recommendations based on reviewing feedback of more than thousand of people who agreed to participate in the author’s little experiment.

If you’d like to know the gist of the routine recommended by the authors then here it is:

  1. Remove all the tech, save for the critical ones to your life or job, for the 30 days.
  2. Have a list of activities to fill the freed time with. Don’t let yourself get in boredom. Either reconnect with what have sparkled joy in you before or find a new ones.
  3. After 30 days reintroduce tech in your life, one piece at a time. But do it only if it’is aligned with your core values.
